About Us

Diamond Bank Plc began as a private limited liability company on March 21,1991(the company was in corporated on December 20,1990). Ten years later ,in february 2001,It became a Universal Bank. 

    In january 2005.following a highly sucessful private placement share which substantially raised the bank's equity base,Diamond Bank became a public limited company.

    In May 2005,the bank was listed on The Nigerian Stock Exchange.Moreover, in January 2008,Diamond Bank's Global Depositary Reciept (GDR) was listed on the professional securities Market of  the London Stock Exchange.The first  Bank to record that feat.
